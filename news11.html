2025 September Premium Gift Gacha

Our new Premium Gift Gacha is here! This gacha comes with two costumes. One for male and one for female! Check out the full details below.

Premium Gift Gacha

Event Duration: 9/13 12:00 PM - 9/30 11:59 AM (PT) / 9/13 19:00 - 9/30 18:59 (UTC) The new costumes will be available for a limited time for 3,000 Paid Crystals! Pulling will give you the new male costume and the new female costume. These new costumes have 4 color variations in total.


If you're curious about how they look, then check them out by going in-game and clicking Costumes and activating the Dress-up Mode option! All new costumes can be found at the bottom of the list.


Notes:

  • These exclusive Costumes may be made available again in the future.
  • The Premium Gift Gacha requires Paid Crystals.
  • Certain Costumes can only be equipped by certain characters.
  • You can only pull on each gacha one time only!
